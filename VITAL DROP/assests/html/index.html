Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Anyone aged 18-65 who weighs at least 50kg and is in good health can donate blood.

How often can I donate blood?

Healthy individuals can donate blood every 56 days, or up to 6 times a year.

Is donating blood safe?

Yes, donating blood is safe. All needles are sterile, used only once, and then discarded.
